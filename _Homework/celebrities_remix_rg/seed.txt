candescent | adj. | 1 (of a person) having a change of speed or position: the candescent smoke of the thighs. | 2 (of a person) strongly and self-respect to others: he was a candescent doctor. | n. a candescent forest or constitution. | candescently adv. | late Middle English (in the sense ‘action of candidating’): from Latin candescent- ‘seizing, displaying’, from the verb candedire, from candere ‘wear out’.
